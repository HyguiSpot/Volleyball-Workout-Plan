Recovery & Facilities Protocol

You have access to premium facilities. Using them correctly is the difference between chronic joint pain and increasing your vertical jump pain-free.

🌊 Aquatic Therapy (Day 3 & Day 6)

1. Active Recovery Swim

Action: 15-20 minutes of light freestyle swimming.
Why: Decompresses the spine, flushes lactic acid, provides zero-impact cardiovascular work.

2. Underwater Resistance

Action: High knees, lateral shuffles, and backward walking in chest-deep water (10 mins).
Why: Builds functional strength and mobility in hips/knees without eccentric loading (joint wear).

🌡 Sauna Protocol

Action: Post-workout, 10-15 minutes max. Hydrate heavily before and after.
Why: Increases blood plasma volume (endurance), releases heat shock proteins (recovery), and relaxes hyper-tonic muscles (especially calves and lower back).

🚞 Jacuzzi & Jets

Action: 10 mins. Target underwater jets specifically on: Patellar tendon (below knee), Achilles tendon, and lower back.
Why: Mechanical massage promotes localized blood flow to avascular areas (tendons), speeding up tissue repair from jumping.

Mandatory Daily Mobility (Pre/Post Workout)

Ankle Dorsiflexion

Knee-to-wall stretch. Crucial for landing mechanics and preventing knee pain. 2x60s per leg.

Hip Flexor / Couch Stretch

Opens tight hips from jumping/sitting. Allows glutes to fire properly for max vertical. 2x60s per leg.

Thoracic Extension

Foam roller upper back. Prevents shoulder impingement during spiking/blocking. 2 mins.
